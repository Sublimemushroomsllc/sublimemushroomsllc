Lion’s Mane

Hericium erinaceus

Formats Available

  • Fresh: $8 per 0.5 lb
  • Powder: $28 per 4 oz
  • Tincture: $20 per 2 oz

Usage

Shreds like crab meat — great for vegan 'crab cakes'. Supports memory and focus.

Storage & Preparation

Keep fresh in a paper bag in the fridge for up to 5 days.

Fun Fact + Medicinal Property

Lion’s Mane promotes nerve regeneration and brain health.

Recipe Idea
