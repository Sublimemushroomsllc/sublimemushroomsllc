Simple Mushroom Recipe Ideas

Lion’s Mane Crab Cakes

Shred Lion’s Mane, mix with breadcrumbs, vegan mayo, Old Bay, and pan-fry.

Reishi Immunity Tea

Simmer dried reishi with cinnamon, ginger, and lemon for 45 minutes.

Chestnut Mushroom Risotto

Sauté chestnuts in olive oil, add arborio rice, veggie broth, garlic, and finish with parsley.
